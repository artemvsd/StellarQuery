FAQ

What is the difference between Stellar Quest Live and Stellar Quest Learn?

Stellar Quest Learn quests are always available for you to complete (you can even do them multiple times for more practice)!
Stellar Quest Live quests are live and timed events that are hosted periodically. Keep your eyes open for live quest announcements!

How much do I need to know about Stellar or blockchain technology to use Stellar Quest Learn?

Very little to get started! Stellar Quest Learn is designed to help both beginners and advanced people explore the Stellar network. You’ll learn as you work through the quests, and any questions you have can be asked in our Stellar Quest Discord.

Do I need a crypto wallet to participate in Stellar Quest Learn?

You don’t need a wallet to complete quests in Stellar Quest Learn! However, if you want to collect unique NFT badges or XLM rewards, you’ll need to set up or connect your Albedo wallet. Want to learn about how to set up a wallet? Check out our Pioneer Quest before diving into level one.

Can I earn XLM in Stellar Quest Learn or Stellar Quest Live?

Yes! You can earn XLM rewards as you complete Stellar Quest Learn levels and during the Stellar Quest Live quest events.
